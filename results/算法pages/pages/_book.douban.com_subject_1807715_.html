    看到这越发觉得这书和technical leader关系太抽象，因为书中总结的东西对所有人都有用。
    1.self-blindness：the number one obstacle
    2.no-problem syndrome：the number two obstacle
    3.single-solution belief：the number three obstacle
    感觉除了3和思维方式相关，另外两个都和个人成熟度有关。self-blindness的人最无语，无论你如何开导他，他看到的世界永远和你不同，这也许就是所谓的悟性吧。不过我是相信人的内心都可以成长的，只要敢于面对自己的懦弱，拥抱你所恐惧的地方。
    no-problem的人过去也许有过成功的经验，但是没有stay foolish，任何时候耐心的听别人把自己的问题讲完，确认自己理解了问题。否则这样的人如果做事情到最后全部返工，可想多大的代价。
    single-solution belief的人也许是深受应试教育的毒害，缺少了innovative和critical thinking的思维。这不要紧，工作中慢慢总结和体会会有所改变。

    2012-05-28 09:33

    ==========
    Becoming a Technical Leader (Gerald Weinberg)
    | Added on Tuesday, May 01, 2012, 10:36 PM
    ==========
    the new leader needs new powers just when technical power is about to slip away.
    ==========
    How much power do you lose from your age, height, sex, skin color, language, religion, attractiveness, education, or personal habits? What can you do about it?
    ==========
    The leader's job is usually not to solve a single problem, but to create an environment in which many problems will be solved, not just for today, but for the future.
    ==========
    Organizing is not about solving problems, but avoiding them.
    ==========
    "They're all doing the best they can, under the circumstances. If I don't think they are doing the best they can, then I don't understand the circumstances."
    ==========
    "I've finally learned that you must let them make mistakes. It's part of the price you pay, and it's more efficient in the long run."
    ==========
    "If you can't even manage your own affairs, what makes you think you can manage others?"
    ==========
    People without vision don't have much influence on other people. Visions are contagious. Even misguided visions, like Hitler's, can sweep people along in their path. If people share your vision, you can lead each other to great accomplishments. The same cannot be said for most of the other reasons people want to be leaders. Only your mother cares if you get rich or famous.
    ==========
    If the job isn't highly technical, the leader need not be competent, but can lead by fear.
    ==========
    If you are a leader, the people are your work. There is no other work worth doing.
    ==========
    Most people understand that helpers are selfish, but also think they are exceptions to the rule.
    ==========
    Attempts to help are often interpreted as attempts to interfere.
    ==========
    No matter how strange it may look, most people are actually trying to be helpful.
    ==========
    Offer to help your neighbor only if you would want to be helped in the same situation, and do it in the way you would want to be helped.
    ==========
    Changes in your support system are seldom painless, but if you intend to grow, you cannot avoid some pain. Several times in my life, I've had a particularly wonderful relationship that I wanted to freeze, so it would never change. Each time I did that, I killed the relationship: The best relationships have been the ones with people who wanted both of us to grow, even if it was sometimes difficult.
    ==========
    Rosy's Response: If you want something that badly, perhaps you shouldn't have it.
    ==========
